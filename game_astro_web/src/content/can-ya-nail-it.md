## Description
Can Ya Nail it is a game where you have to answer 15 questions to become a millionaire! But don't worry because it's not a ripoff of WWTBAM as there is a completely original lifeline combination and there's also 4 instead of the usual 3!

<br>

### How to Play

Click on the answer you think is correct, but be careful - one strike and you're out!
