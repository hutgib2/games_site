## Description
Vampire Survivor is an endless game where you shoot bats, blobs, skeletons and demon bosses. 
Try to survive for as long as possible, while collecting fun powerups to help you stay alive.
You have 3 lives before you die, the aim is to collect as many points as you can by killing enemies before the innevitable happens...


## How to Play
- Use arrows keys to move 
- click the mouse to fire.
- move your mouse around to aim your gun.

## Powerups
- **Super-speed**: Increase movement speed x3.
- **Slow zone**: Slows down enemies near player.
- **Shotgun**: Gun fires additonal diagonal shots.
- **Machine gun**: Increase fire rate x3. All powerups last 5 seconds.
- **Sideshot**: Fires bullets in all directions.
- **Spawn stop**: Temporarily delay the spawning of enemies.
- **Lasergun**: Enemies spawn a laser that damagto the side where they were shot upon death.
- **Shield**: Unable to take damage for 5 seconds.
- **Sword**: Can kill every unit it touches except for the boss.
